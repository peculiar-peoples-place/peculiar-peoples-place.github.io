About Peculiar Genz

We are a vibrant community of young people passionate about living for Christ. As part of The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) under The Kings Royal Palace (KRP), we exist to raise a generation that is peculiar — set apart for God's purpose.

Our mission is simple: to lead young people into a genuine relationship with Jesus Christ and help them grow in faith, purpose, and character.
